Your contract and checkout experience may be provided by a payment reseller. If you are not satisfied with your first purchase of a given Arcade Errors tier (Starter Pack, Full Bundle, or Agency License), you may request a full refund within 30 calendar days of the date of that purchase’s payment.

3. After 30 days

After the 30-day window, we generally do not provide refunds for digital products that have been delivered, except where required by law or where we agree otherwise in writing.

  1. Email support@4rce.com with the email address used at checkout, product name (Starter / Full / Agency), and approximate purchase date; or
  2. Contact your payment provider’s buyer support (Paddle or Lemon Squeezy) using the details on your receipt.

We aim to respond within a few business days. Paddle and other processors typically credit refunds to the original payment method; timing depends on banks and payment methods (often several business days).

We take fraud and policy abuse seriously. We may refuse service or decline refunds where we detect abuse (for example repeated purchase-and-refund cycles without legitimate cause), breach of our Terms of Service, or fraudulent chargebacks.
